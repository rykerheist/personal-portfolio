About Me

Hi, I'm John Willsdon, founder of Disruptive Learning Design. I'm an instructional designer, facilitator, and a firm believer that learning shouldn't feel like a soul-crushing chore.

Let's be honest, we've all been there: clicking through endless slides of mind-numbing training content, feeling our will to live slowly ebb away. It's like being trapped in a digital purgatory, where the "Next" button is the only glimmer of hope. I've felt that pain, that existential dread of another pointless training module. And I decided I wasn't going to take it anymore. I made it my life's work to rescue learners from this digital abyss and inject some much-needed life back into learning.

While other instructional designers were busy alphabetizing their font collections, I was in the trenches, figuring out how to create experiences that people actually enjoyed and that demonstrably improved their skills. I've spent years honing my craft, experimenting with unconventional techniques (like that time I used a blindfolded, trust-building exercise to help a team overcome communication barriers and achieve a seemingly impossible goal, the office bully even became a team player!) and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the world of learning.

I'm a student of human behavior, both in life and academia, which means I can not only tell you why your $800,000, 3-day leadership offsite failed to move the needle on any of your performance metrics but also back it up with scientific evidence. My expertise in human motivation means I can help you see that sales training alone won't lead to double-digit profit growth, and then show you what will work. (Hint: it involves understanding what truly motivates your people.) For example, by implementing a gamified learning platform for a recent client, we saw a 30% increase in course completion rates and a 15% improvement in knowledge retention.

What Makes Me Tick?

  • Embracing Technology to Drive Results: This is an incredibly exciting time to be in learning. I'm driven to innovate and discover what's possible, leveraging the latest tech to create impactful experiences for your learners. For example, we can now build a virtual reality experience with an AI-powered role-play partner that enables your team to hone their negotiation skills in a safe, realistic environment. And yes, the system can automatically adjust the difficulty level as it tracks and evaluates each learner's progress. For your learners this means they can practice in a realistic environment and master skills faster.
  • Allergic to Boredom, Addicted to Engagement: If I catch even a whiff of dull, yawn-inducing content, I break out in hives. My mission is to make learning as binge-worthy as the most addictive TV series. Remember the anticipation for the next episode of Melrose Place? That's the level of excitement I aim to create for your learners.
  • The Power of Possibility: I believe that anyone can learn anything with the right approach. By understanding individual learning styles and motivations, I create tailored experiences that unlock each learner's potential.

Working with me might result in: a noticeable increase in learner engagement and knowledge retention, a significant boost in the ROI of your training programs, and a newfound enthusiasm for learning within your organization. You might even catch your employees having fun while they learn. Consider yourself warned.

Our Design Process

Analyse

Let's dissect this beast. We're not just poking around here; we're performing a full-on autopsy of your learning needs. We'll dig deep into the guts of your organisation, figure out what makes your learners tick (or not tick), and identify the root of your training problems. No stone unturned, no sacred cow un-tipped. We'll figure out what your learners need, even if they don't know themselves.

Design

Time to cook up some learning magic. This is where we take all that juicy data we unearthed and turn it into a blueprint for a learning experience so potent it should come with a warning label. We're talking mind-bending scenarios, gamification that's actually addictive, and content so engaging it'll make your learners forget they're supposed to be learning. We'll make it so good they'll be begging to be trained.

Develop

Unleashing the Kraken of creation. We're building your learning experience, and it's going to be a monster (the good kind). Think interactive simulations that feel more real than reality, videos that'll grab your audience's eyeballs, and content so sharp it could cut glass. This is where our twisted genius comes to life. We're not just building training; we're crafting an experience.

Implement

Let's shove this thing into the real world and see if it explodes. Time to release our creation into the wild. We'll roll out your training with the precision of a military operation and the finesse of a... well, you get the idea. We'll set the stage for maximum impact, get everyone fired up, then launch this thing like a rocket. If your audience was unmotivated before, they won't be after.

Evaluate

Did we break your learners' brains... or fix them? Time to find out. We're not afraid to face the music. We'll meticulously measure the impact of your training, scrutinize the data like a hawk, and figure out what worked, what didn't, and what we can make even more mind-blowingly awesome next time. We'll see what got them hooked and what we can do to make them never want to leave. The goal is to do better than perfect, we want to blow their expectations out of the water.
